About the Event

The WIMIn Ideathon is a two-day event aiming to connect, inspire, and explore the potential of women across disciplines while helping them seed or grow ideas.

March 5 and 6, 2016, at the University of Waterloo.

Our Commitment

WIMIn is proud to be a part of the University of Waterloo's official support for HeForShe, the UN Women's Solidarity Movement for gender equality.

Learn more about HeForShe here.

Is your idea daunting because you need help from outside your area of study or experience? Do you want to generate an idea or acquire new skills? Learn how at WIMIn.

Network

Get your foot in the door by getting to know students, staff, and faculty from across campus. Connect with like-minded women and grow your professional or social circles at WIMIn.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is WIMIn?

Waterloo Women: Ideas, Makers, Innovators is a two-day event for women at the University of Waterloo. The first day is a conference, which later transitions into a team-based contest called an ideathon. The event is on March 5 and 6, so mark your calendar and RSVP now!

What is an Ideathon?

Our ideathon is a team-based creative challenge where groups of attendees work together to collaborate on a project of their choosing. These projects are not limited to one area of expertise, and can take full advantage of our cross-faculty audience.

Who can attend?

All members of the University of Waterloo community (including students, alumni, faculty, and staff) who identify as a woman or as non-gender-binary are welcome to attend.

I am a male. Can I attend?

Sorry, but this event is all about our #WaterlooWomen! Please share your support by inviting your female friends on Facebook or by following us on Twitter.

What will it cost me?

WIMIn is completely free to attend! Come and enjoy the event and the food, it's on us. Accommodations and transportation to and from the event are your responsibility, though.

Do I need a ticket?

It is an open registration, so tickets aren't needed. RSVP here and you're good to go!

What if I don't have an idea?

You will find plenty of inspiration during the conference on the first day of the event. Keep in mind that you can work on any idea you have - start a blog, create a business, paint a mural, plan an event of your own - the possibilities are endless!

What if I don't have a team?

No worries! We will help you find teammates during the first day of our event. Feel free to post on our event page on Facebook to find a team before the event, too.
