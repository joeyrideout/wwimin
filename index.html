About the Event

The WWIMIn Conference is a two-day event aiming to connect, inspire, and explore the potential of women across disciplines while helping them seed or grow ideas.

March 5 and 6, 2016, at the University of Waterloo.

Our Commitment

WWIMIn is proud to be a part of the University of Waterloo's official support for HeForShe, the UN Women's Solidarity Movement for gender equality.

Learn more about HeForShe here.

Grow

Utilize resources and mentors across campus who want to help you grow. Discover how the University of Waterloo can help take you to new heights.

Learn

Is your idea daunting because you need help from outside your area of study or experience? Do you want to generate an idea or acquire new skills? Learn how at WWIMIn.

Network

Get your foot in the door by getting to know students, staff, and faculty from across campus. Connect with like-minded women and grow your professional or social circles at WWIMIn.

Seed an Idea

Participate in idea-generation workshops and learn about the process of creating great ideas that are worth growing. Talk to entrepreneurial women from around campus about where they got their ideas.

Grow an Idea

Utilize the academic, professional, and social resources of the University of Waterloo and take your idea to the next level. Participate in workshops, find a team of like-minded women to work with, and see how far your idea can go.

Get free things

Free food and goodie bags will be given to all attendees! And yes - the t-shirts will come in ladies cuts.
